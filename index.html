"We don't just serve food, we create experiences that keep our customers coming back."

  • Flame-grilled perfection since 2020
  • Family-friendly with kids' menu and high chairs
  • Options for all diets including vegan and gluten-free

Benitos started in 2020 in Mamelodi, founded by Tshepo Mokoena and Joseph Mathe, with a simple goal, to make tasty, affordable kasi food accessible without needing to travel. In 2021, they moved to Olivenhoutbosch and have been growing ever since.

From just one employee back then to a team of 19 today, Benitos is doing its part to fight unemployment in the community. Known for their popular garlic Russians, homemade spices, and chillis, Benitos has become more than a food spot, it’s a place of warmth and support. Many come in for a meal and leave with a smile, feeling cared for.

At Benitos, it’s not just about food, it’s about feeding the soul and uplifting the kasi.

Why Choose Benitos

We stand out from typical fast food with our commitment to quality, variety, and that authentic flame-grilled taste that keeps our customers coming back week after week.
